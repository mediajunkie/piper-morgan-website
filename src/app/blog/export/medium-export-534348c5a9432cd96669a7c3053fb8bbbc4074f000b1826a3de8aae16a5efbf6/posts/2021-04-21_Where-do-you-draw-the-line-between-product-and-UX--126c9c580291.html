Where do you draw the line between product management and user experience?

This is really three questions in one:

  1. First, it’s a question I am asking you. Where does one role end and the other begin in your shop? Is it a settled issue? How well is it working out?
  2. Second, it’s an interview question most product, UX, and design practitioners will encounter as they seek opportunities in new environments and take on greater responsibilities.
  3. Finally, it’s a question every product leader has to answer for their own team, mission, and organization. Does UX report to product or are they peers? Who is running this workshop? etc.

In every case it comes down to the specific people you’re working with, their mix of talents, skills, and experience:

The challenge for a leader is cultivating talent, hiring complementary skills, instilling product mindset, and building a product-centric culture.

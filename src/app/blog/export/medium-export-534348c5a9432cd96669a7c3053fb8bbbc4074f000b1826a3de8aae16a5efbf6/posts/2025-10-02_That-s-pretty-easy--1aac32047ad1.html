That's pretty easy. I decided since it's an AI series to do the "obvious" thing and ask ChatGPT to make cartoons to illustrate my posts in this series.

When I write each first draft I try to think up a visual metaphor involving people and robots doing stuff together that relates to the topic, so like the recent one about "doing the deep work" I thought a diving bell and robot and human diver on the ocean floor.

I went to ChatGPT and prompted "Please draw me a landscape format cartoon of a robot and a person exploring the ocean floor after descending in a deep-sea diving bell" and voila.

The consistency of style comes from not specifying any style (most of the time) and getting ChatGPT's current "generic" cartoon style (though you can see it gradually evolving over the course of the series).

Occasionally I do one-offs in other styles (Tolkein-ish, Noir), etc., when it fits the episode.

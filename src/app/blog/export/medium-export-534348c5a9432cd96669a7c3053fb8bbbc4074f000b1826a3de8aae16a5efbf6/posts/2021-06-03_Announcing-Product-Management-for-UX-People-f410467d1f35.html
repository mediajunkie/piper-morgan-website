Announcing Product Management for UX People

Towards the end of this year Rosenfeld Media will be publishing my book Product Management for UX People: From Designing to Thriving in a Product World (you may sign up there to be notified when it is available for order), the culmination of a multiple-year project that has unfolded with the input and support of the Design in Product community.

In the weeks and months leading up to this publication date, I will be sharing initial chapter drafts for public review and feedback, in parallel with the more formal* editorial and publishing process. As I do, I’ll make the links in this current draft outline (subject to re-factoring in the developmental edit process) available at a pace of roughly one a week, inviting feedback and discussion to help me strengthen the material before printing it onto dead trees.
